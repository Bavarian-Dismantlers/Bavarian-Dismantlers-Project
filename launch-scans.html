Diagnostic Scans

Original and final scan reports taken with Launch X431. Captured shortly after acquisition, with the vehicle in running condition. These reports show baseline faults present prior to any dismantling work.

2014 Porsche Panamera S E-Hybrid

  • View Original Scan
  • View Pre-Tear-Down Scan

Final scan contains TPM and PASM faults. This is because the car had the original air suspension removed in favor of coilovers. These coilovers are NOT OEM and will be clearly documented as such in the listing. Wheels contain working TPM sensors, but system was turned off and never re-activated at the dealer. Re-coding is required.

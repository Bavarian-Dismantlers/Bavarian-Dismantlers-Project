Who We Are

Bavarian Dismantlers is a father-and-son automotive recycling project based in Colorado, focused on tearing down German vehicles and re-homing OEM parts from brands like Porsche, BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz, and Volkswagen. Every component we sell is backed by photo evidence, video walkarounds, and diagnostic logs so you know exactly what you’re getting.

This digital portfolio is more than just a pile of images & links. It is a traceable record of each vehicle’s condition from first cold start to final teardown. Serving as the photo companion to the Bavarian Dismantlers Youtube channel. A simple, easy to navigate archive of every aspect of the car. Dissassembly will be documented and added to the website as it's being completed. Our goal is to make buying used OEM parts as predictable and trustworthy as buying new.

As we expand, future projects will follow the same standard: clear documentation, organized media, and honest representation of every part we pull. Together with the Youtube channel, this forms the full digital portfolio behind our listings—keeping everything transparent, documented, and easy to reference.
